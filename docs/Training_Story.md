# Training Scenario Account Structure

Account hierarchy across the two apps. Each bank is a separate institution
with its own ledger structure; the training stories will follow money
flowing within and between them.

```
Sasquatch National Bank
 ├── Bigfoot Brews
 ├── Sasquatch Sips
 ├── Yeti Espresso
 ├── FEB Corporate Bank
 │    ├── Valley Grain Co-op
 │    └── Harvest Credit Exchange
 └── FEB Consumer Bank
      ├── Big Meadow Checking
      └── Harvest Moon Savings
```
