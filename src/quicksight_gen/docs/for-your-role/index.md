# For Your Role

Five role-oriented entry points into the dashboards. Pick yours;
the page funnels you into the right
[Reference](../reference/index.md),
[Walkthroughs](../walkthroughs/index.md), and
[Concepts](../concepts/index.md) — and tells you what *not* to
spend time learning.

Each page follows the same shape:

- **What you do today** — the current pain.
- **What this tool does differently** — the value prop.
- **What we are *not* asking you to learn** — scope reassurance.
- **How to start** — numbered onboarding steps.
- **The concepts you'll want grounded** — links into Concepts.
- **What "good" looks like** — the acceptance bar.

## Pick your role

- [For the operator](operator.md) — primary user of the L1
  Reconciliation Dashboard; runs the morning routine, finds
  exceptions, drills to root cause.
- [For the integrator](integrator.md) — owns the L2 institution
  YAML; uses L2 Flow Tracing to prove declarations match runtime
  reality.
- [For the ETL engineer](etl-engineer.md) — owns the projection
  from upstream source systems into the two shared base tables.
- [For the executive](executive.md) — checks Money Moved /
  Transaction Volume / Account Coverage at weekly + monthly +
  quarterly cadences.
- [For the compliance analyst](compliance-analyst.md) — uses the
  Investigation dashboard for AML triage and SAR-evidence pulls.
